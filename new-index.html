Namaste!

Dr Om Murti Anil is a senior interventional cardiologist working at Grande International Hospital and Heart Clinic. He is first DM cardiologist from AIIMS, New Delhi. He is known in the country not only for his work in cardiac intervention, but also for his contribution in the field of prevention of heart disease in Nepal & public awareness activities. He has also contributed in the field of research by conducting one of the largest study in Nepal involving over 5500 apparently healthy individuals. He is author of a very popular and health informative book "Ma Pani Doctor".

Dr.Om Murti Anil was born in a small village (Dhamaura) of Mahottari district in Nepal. Although he was born in lower middle socioeconomy family, his moral standards had been always high.He had great determination to become a doctor since his childhood. Read More.
